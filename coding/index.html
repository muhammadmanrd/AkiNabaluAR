Sinopsis Cerita / Synopsis

Tukar bahasa dan baca ringkasan cerita.

Aki Nabalu ialah seorang lelaki tua yang bijak dan penyayang, tinggal di kaki Gunung Nabalu. Dia menjaga hutan, mendengar sungai, dan mengajar kanak-kanak menghormati haiwan dan tumbuhan. Suatu hari, tanda-tanda aneh muncul seperti langit gelap, haiwan lari, dan angin bertiup kuat. Aki tahu ada sesuatu yang tidak kena dengan alam di kampung mereka. Dia masuk ke hutan dan melihat sungai tercemar, pokok tumbang, dan hutan rosak. Menyedari roh gunung sedang sedih, Aki memulakan pendakian ke puncak gunung. Di perjalanan, dia menghadapi kabus tebal, pokok hidup, dan ilusi yang menguji keberaniannya. Dengan hati yang tulus, Aki sampai ke puncak dan bertemu roh gunung. Roh itu berkata manusia telah lupa menjaga alam, dan Aki berjanji akan membantu menenangkan gunung.

Aki Nabalu is a wise and caring old man who lives at the foot of Mount Nabalu. He looks after the forest, listens to the rivers, and teaches children to respect animals and plants. One day, strange signs appear, such as dark skies, animals fleeing, and strong winds blowing. Aki knows that something is wrong with nature in their village. He enters the forest and sees polluted rivers, fallen trees, and a damaged forest. Realizing that the mountain spirit is sad, Aki begins his journey to climb to the mountainâ€™s peak. Along the way, he faces thick mist, living trees, and illusions that test his courage. With a sincere heart, Aki reaches the summit and meets the mountain spirit. The spirit tells him that humans have forgotten how to care for nature, and Aki promises to help calm the mountain.
